Lan Gaming

EVENT RULES

1. Punckbusters are enables, so any use of cheats or trainers will result in immediate disqualification.
2. Two games have been decided for the competition i.e. Far cry 2 (First Person Shooting) & Blur (Arcade Racing)
3. The final round shall be of Hard Core Mode, i.e. there will be no powerups or mods available in blur, and there will be put limitations on the allowance for buying weapons in Far cry 2,
4. Maps will be decided be voting system which are inbuilt in the game except for the first round.
5. Participants will be allowed to take two trials just to get familiar with the controls and working of game.
6. Decisions of the Head coordinator will be final.
7. Each participant will at least compete (two times) * in both the games, if the no. of participants exceeded 24, then blur game will decide the qualifiers for the final round i.e. Far Cry 2.
8. Entry fee for induvial participant is Rs. 50/=

Fun Hunter

EVENT RULES

1. Two member per team.
2. Prelims will be conducted.
3. The Treasure Hunt consists of 5-7clues, each one leading to the next clue. Each correctly solved clue carries 10 points. And final Treasure carries 50 points.
4. The clues are to be found in a particular order.
5. A team cannot skip a clue.
6. Skipping a clue leads to disqualification of the team.
7. Do not search for clues in off-limit areas.
8. If you found other team’s clue by any chance never tamper it. A violation of this rule could cause disqualification of your team.
9. You move any object to search the clue, kindly replace it back.
10. All the clues of the teams are different and located at different places but the final destination i.e. the treasure is same. The team which finds the treasure wins first.
11. IF u find more than one chits (which contains clue) at same place .take only one. Otherwise team will be disqualified.
